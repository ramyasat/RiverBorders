I am Ramya, a PhD candidate in History at Michigan State. I study river borders, specifically, the Detroit River which forms the border between the Canada and United States. I study the effects of dredging on the river. This website is my attempt to offer a one-stop source for all river borders. I hope educators can use this website to help their students get a sense of river borders across the world, their scale and sheer numbers.
